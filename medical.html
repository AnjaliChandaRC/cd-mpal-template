CDMpal: Medical Software For Individuals and Families

CDMpal is not just another App, it is a mini medical software specially designed for non-medical Individuals and families so they can keep, track, and produce vital health information anywhere anytime.

Health Summary

So your patients are always prepared for travel, emergencies and dealing with multiple practitioners.

Mednotes

So your patients can create their brief notes on treatments, procedures, instructions and any important health events.

Diary

So your patients can log, track and report symptoms, pain, blood pressure, diet, weight and blood sugar series.

Documents

So your patients can keep important test results, reports, ECGs, legal documents and all in one place.

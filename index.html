Why Do Patients Need CDMpal?

Over 45% of Patient Concerns

are never disclosed or discussed with their healthcare practitioners*

This can lead to misunderstanding, misdiagnosis and less than optional care delivery all leading to patient-practitioner frustration wasting time and money!

Incomplete History

People forget symptoms, medical concerns, health events, treatments and more.

The time can be wasted in searching, validating and chasing information.

Active Participation Is Always Better

When people have active input into their clinical care, there is higher engagement, fewer follow-up failures, and more patient accountability as they feel part of the solution, not just passive followers of instructions.

CDMpal: Medical Software For Individuals and Families

CDMpal is not just another App, it is a mini medical software specially designed for non-medical Individuals and families so they can keep, track, and produce vital health information anywhere anytime.

Health Summary

So your patients are always prepared for travel, emergencies and dealing with multiple practitioners.

Mednotes

So your patients can create their brief notes on treatments, procedures, instructions and any important health events.

Diary

So your patients can log, track and report symptoms, pain, blood pressure, diet, weight and blood sugar series.

Documents

So your patients can keep important test results, reports, ECGs, legal documents and all in one place.

FAQs

Is CDMpal secure?

Absolutely, We prioritise your privacy and security. CDMpal uses bank-level encryption to protect your personal and medical information, ensuring it remains confidential and secure. More importantly, your information is yours and remains with you.

Where is data stored?

Your data is stored in AWS cloud storage facilities in Australia that are compliant with the highest data security standards and frameworks such as ISO 27017 for cloud security, ISO 27701 for privacy information management and ISO 27018 for cloud privacy.

Do you need Patient Health Identification in CDMpal?

CDMpal does not require any personal identifications making it one of the most secure systems available.
